How GPA is Calculated

To calculate your GPA, you need to provide the subject name, credit hours, and grade points achieved. The GPA is calculated using the formula:

GPA = (Total Grade Points Earned) / (Total Credit Hours)

Examples:

Subject Credit Hours Grade Points
Mathematics34.00
Physics43.70
Chemistry23.30

Total Grade Points = (3x4.00) + (4x3.70) + (2x3.30) = 33.10

Total Credit Hours = 3 + 4 + 2 = 9

GPA = 33.10 / 9 = 3.68
